Lazy Cake, the pseudo cake everyone loves!

Description:

A no-bake dessert that's as easy as it is delicious.

This indulgent treat combines layers of chocolate, biscuit, and creamy goodness to create a dessert everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 200 g digestive biscuits (or any other biscuits of choice)
  • 100 g cocoa powder
  • 200 g butter
  • 200 g condensed milk
  • 100 g sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. Break the digestive biscuits into small pieces (you can crush them by hand or use a rolling pin).
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat, then add sugar, cocoa powder, and condensed milk. Stir continuously to combine.
  3. Once the mixture is well combined and smooth, remove from the heat and stir in vanilla extract (if using).
  4. Pour the chocolate mixture over the crushed biscuits and stir until well coated.
  5. Transfer the biscuit-chocolate mixture into a lined baking tin and press it down firmly to compact the mixture.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours or until it has set firmly.
  7. Once chilled, cut into squares and serve!

Enjoy you meal!
